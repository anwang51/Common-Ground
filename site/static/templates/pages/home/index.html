Project Overview

Take a look at our projects this semester.

Imagination Networks

Incorporating imagination augmented agents into managerial networks

The goal of this reinforcement learning project is to empower artificial intelligence models with the ability to both act at different temporal resolutions as well as enabling an actor at each level to make long term strategic policy decisions.

3D Facial Reconstruction

Generating 3 dimensional models of human faces from low fidelity imaging

This project will work to improve upon current domain-specific 3d model generation by exploring the use of different statistical and deep learning preprocessing techniques, using and modifying convolutional modules, and altering low level 3 dimensional model representations.

Object Tracking Through Time

Detecting and persistently tracking objects in a real time video stream

Unique objects will be assigned unique IDs, which will persist though translations and rotations of the objects in the video data. Another goal will be to continue tracking and remember IDs across multiple video streams or upon exiting and reentering the camera’s field of view.

Multi-Objective Robotics

Tackling real-world problems with conflicting objectives by simultaneously and independently optimizing all solutions on the Pareto front

This project will investigate how to implement and improve the existing MO-CMA-ES algorithm to operate a Baxter robot quickly and adaptively in production for a multi-objective problem such as collision avoidance.

Jazz Improvisations

Creating models to generate jazz melodies in specified styles

This project will explore several approaches to melody generation, including Markov models, clustering methods, and LSTM neural networks. The general approach will attempt to strike a balance between novelty and coherence by utilizing “note categories” and melodic abstraction techniques.
